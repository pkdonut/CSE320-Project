Usage

To search for a product, click on the small magnifying glass icon and
enter the name of the product you would like to search for

Making an account

Making an account can be useful if you would like to save
searches and comparisons for later. Accounts require an e-mail address.

To make an account, scroll to the bottom of the home page and click
on "Sign up". This should bring you to a page where you can enter
your e-mail address and make a username
